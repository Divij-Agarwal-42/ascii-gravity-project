Interactive ASCII Gravity Simulator

Reimagining fluid motion with ASCII characters

Imagine a mesmerizing program that employs intricate fluid motion code to transform static text into a dynamic, fluidic masterpiece. This innovative software harnesses the power of complex algorithms to breathe life into text, infusing it with fluid-like behaviors that defy convention. As the words flow and ripple across the screen, they take on an almost tangible quality, resembling liquid streams that gracefully adapt to their surroundings. With an artistic touch, this program turns typography into a captivating dance of colors, shapes, and movements, offering a unique and visually stunning way to convey messages and captivate audiences. Whether used for artistic expression or interactive user interfaces, this program represents a harmonious blend of technology and creativity, making text come alive like never before.

A Quick Introduction To How The Program Works:

Welcome to the ASCII gravity simulator! Begin by entering the text you want to be displayed up to 6 alphabet characters. Then, enter the colors that compose the background and text displayed. To test if your input was valid, press the "Draw Initial Image". To animate, press the "Animate" button. The animation will then begin to play. The default direction of the characters is down, but toggling through the gravity options can modify the direction the characters flow in. The gravity speed toggle can be used to modify the speed of the character flow. When you are satisfied with the animation, press the "Reload Animation Canvas" to reset the canvas and animation options. Enjoy!
